The Volvo Charge Club Experience

Gamified Rewards Program

Strategy, UI, User Testing, Handoff

The Volvo Charge Club is a program developed to integrate into the existing Volvo Cars app, aiming to encourage Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicle (PHEV) owners to utilize their vehicles’ electric capabilities more frequently. Research showed that many drivers rely on gasoline instead of charging their cars, missing out on the environmental and financial benefits of clean energy. The program’s goal is to incentivize users to adopt sustainable charging habits by introducing features like gamified rewards, improved app functionality, and seamless backend integration.

During the discovery phase, we conducted user research, analyzed customer feedback, and reviewed competitor offerings to identify key pain points, such as delayed data processing and inconsistent app performance. These insights informed the design of a solution that not only motivates users to charge their vehicles but also aligns with Volvo’s values of sustainability, innovation, and customer satisfaction. By enhancing the app experience, the Volvo Charge Club aims to support eco-friendly driving while improving the overall ownership journey for PHEV drivers.

UX Exploration

With the premise of creating a gamified experience to help drivers develop more sustainable habits with electric energy, we began by identifying how this feature could seamlessly integrate into the existing Volvo Cars app. Our focus was on mapping out the user journey to ensure the experience felt intuitive and engaging, from discovering weekly challenges to tracking progress and redeeming rewards.

The Volvo Charge Club introduced weekly challenges based on car usage data and user driving habits, identified through research. Users earn points by maximizing clean energy use, with challenges ranging from distance driven and driving efficiency to city exploration and special date events. Points can be redeemed for rewards like Volvo gadgets, recharging discounts, or credits toward electric bills, encouraging sustainable driving while enhancing engagement with the Volvo Cars app.

UI Production

In the UI phase, we translated the wireframes into functional screens while following Volvo’s brand guidelines and imagery. This ensured consistency in design elements such as typography, color palette, and layout, creating a user interface that aligned with the existing app and brand identity.

Key Learnings

  • User research is essential to design challenges and rewards that align with user behaviors and motivations.
  • Meaningful rewards drive engagement, balancing short-term incentives with long-term goals for sustained participation.
  • Data-driven iteration ensures success, allowing continuous refinement of the experience based on user feedback and behavior.
  • Seamless integration critical for adoption: Gamified features must blend naturally into existing platforms to ensure ease of use and minimize disruption to the user experience, encouraging consistent engagement.
