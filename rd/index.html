Resultados Digitais Internal Projects

Digital Marketing and SalesAutomation Platform

Team Management, Design Ops, Branding

Resultados Digitais is a Brazilian company founded in 2011 that specializes in digital marketing and sales automation. It provides a platform for managing online marketing, generating leads, and optimizing sales processes through tools for inbound marketing, email marketing, social media management, CRM, and analytics, primarily targeting small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs).

My time at the company can be divided into three key phases: first, as a solo marketing designer, where I managed all digital assets for the marketing team; second, as a design leader, responsible for establishing and leading the internal Creative Services team to scale design operations as the company expanded; and third, as a DesignOps professional, marking a technical career shift to support more strategic projects within the organization.

Marketing Assets

The Inbound Marketing strategy centered on digital assets like landing pages, eBooks, and webinars, along with in-person events such as RD Summit—the largest marketing event in Brazil—and RD on the Road, a regional traveling event. We created all supporting materials for these initiatives, including event branding, signage, and promotional content, ensuring consistency and a cohesive experience across all channels.

Design Ops

As a Design Ops, my role was to streamline processes, tools, and workflows to ensure the design team operated efficiently and delivered consistent results. I managed resources, facilitated collaboration across departments, ensured consistency across marketing assets, and tracked metrics to optimize performance and scalability while aligning design efforts with broader business goals and supporting the rebranding project.

Team Management

From 2016 to 2020, the Creative Services team operated as an in-house design agency for the RD marketing team. During this time, the team was built and expanded to include 16 professionals, comprising designers, developers, and video production specialists, under dedicated management.

The routine as a design leader included:

  • Providing guidance and support to the design team while keeping a regular 1:1 routine and supporting individual development plans.
  • Overseeing design projects from conception to completion.
  • Assigning tasks and responsibilities based on team members' strengths.
  • Acting as a liaison between the design team and other departments.
  • Developing and implementing design strategies that aligned with business objectives.
  • Ensuring quality assurance by reviewing design work.
  • Supporting the professional growth of team members through mentorship.
  • Encouraging collaboration within the team and with other department.
